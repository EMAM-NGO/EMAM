About Us

11 Years Experience

Emam Foundation for Development and Culture, a UNFCCC-accredited non-profit, is a pivotal force in climate action. Founded in 2015 with a focus on community engagement, it has evolved to send delegations to COP27 and COP28, actively participating in global climate conferences. The foundation's commitment extends to leadership in the Communications department of COY17, showcasing its dedication to youth empowerment. Accreditation by the UNFCCC underscores its credibility and influence on the international stage. As a recognized entity, the Emam Foundation bridges local and global initiatives, contributing significantly to climate policy discussions and environmental advocacy. In 2015, Emam Culture and Development partnered with the Egyptian Ministry of Environment as the sole organization selected for the Egyptian national afforestation project in the vulnerable Nile Delta region.
